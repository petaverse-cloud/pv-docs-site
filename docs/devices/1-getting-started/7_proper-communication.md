# Proper Communication with Your WAGGURACY Devices
 Key steps to ensure proper communication with your WAGGURACY devices:

+ Network Connection: 

	 Ensure that both your **WAGGURACY PHAST 1** and your **smartphone** are **connected** to a stable internet network.

+ Update App and Firmware: 

	Make sure you are using the **latest version** of the WAGGURACY app. Similarly, check for **available firmware updates** for your WAGGURACY PHAST 1. Updating both can resolve compatibility issues.

# Device Activation

 Getting started is easy:
1. [Sign in](/docs/petority/accounts/signing-up) to the WAGGURACY App with your email.
2. Turn on **Bluetooth** and keep your phone close to **PHAST 1** — we’ll find it for you.
3. Confirm eSIM Plan Details & Activation Policy.
4. Set your **home address** as your **home fence** to begin protecting your furry friend.
5. Create your **pet’s profile** to complete the setup and start tracking right away.
6. Wait for the notification that your device has been activated, and you're ready to beign using the WAGGURACY PHAST 1!

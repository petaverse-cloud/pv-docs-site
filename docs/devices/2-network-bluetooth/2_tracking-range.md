# How Far Can It Track My Dog?
WAGGURACY PHAST 1 offers a reliable tracking solution to help you keep tabs on your furry friend, but the tracking range can vary based on factors like network coverage and terrain. 

**Optimal range:**

**1-2 kilometers** from the base station. WAGGURACY PHAST 1 provide reliable real-time pet tracking within this distance.

Here's what you need to know about the tracking range of WAGGURACY PHAST 1:

+ GPS and Network Coverage:

    WAGGURACY PHAST 1 relies on GPS technology and network coverage to provide accurate location information. As long as your pet is within an area with network connectivity, you can track their location. This means that the tracking range can be quite extensive in urban and well-connected areas.
+ Open Spaces vs. Obstacles:
  
    You can expect a broader tracking range in open outdoor spaces, such as parks or fields. However, the tracking range might be limited due to potential signal interference while your pet is indoors or in areas with dense vegetation or many buildings.

+ Real-time Updates:
  
    WAGGURACY PHAST 1 provides real-time updates on your pet's location. This means you can monitor their movements continuously, regardless of the distance, as long as they are inside an area with network converage.

+ Keep in Mind:
  
   It's important to note that WAGGURACY PHAST 1's large tracking range may be limited by the abovementioned factors. Try to ensure your pet's collar or PHAST 1 has a clear line of sight to the sky to receive optimal GPS signals.

WAGGURACY PHAST 1 are designed to help you track your dog's location effectivley, providing peace of mind whether your pet is nearby or exploring a bit farther from home.

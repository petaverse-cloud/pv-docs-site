# Real-Time Pulse Monitoring
WAGGURACY offers a cutting-edge feature that allows you to **monitor your pet's pulse in real time**. This innovative function provides valuable insights into your pet's **habit** and **well-being**, Allowing you to stay on top of their needs.

![Real-Time Pulse](/img/heart/Heart-Rate1.jpg)

## Feature Highlights:
### 1.Habit Insights:
Real-time pulse monitoring offers immediate insights into your pet's habit status. Sudden changes in pulse can signal various habit conditions, enabling you to take prompt action when necessary.
### 2.Early Detection: 
By keeping a vigilant eye on your pet's pulse, you can detect potential habit issues at an early stage. This early detection can lead to timely veterinary care, potentially resulting in better outcomes for your furry companion.
### 3.Personalized Care:
Continuous pulse monitoring allows you to customize your pet's care routine based on their specific needs. Whether it's adjusting exercise levels, ensuring adequate rest, or seeking medical attention, you can make well-informed decisions.
### 4.Remote Monitoring:
Even when you're not physically present, you can stay connected and monitor your pet's pulse. This feature proves especially valuable for busy pet owners or when you're away from home.

Pulse is influenced by factors such as age, habit condition, and activity level. For any concerns, please consult a veterinarian.

When pets exercise vigorously, their pulse goes up to meet the increased demand for blood and oxygen. Research shows their pulse can **rise by about 20%** compared to rest. It's a normal response, helping them adapt to activity levels. So, if your pet's pulse is up during playtime, it's usually nothing to worry about—it's just their body keeping up with the fun!

### How to use:
![steps](/img/heart/Heart-Rate2.jpg)

1. Access the Feature:

    Open the **WAGGURACY app** on your Android or iOS device and log in.
2. Navigate to Pulse: 

    Tap on the **Habit** section in the app's menu.
3. Select Your Pet: 

    If you have multiple pets registered in the app, choose the pet whose pulse you want to monitor.
4. Real-Time Monitoring:

    Once you've selected the pet, you'll be able to see their pulse in real time. The app provides a graphical representation of the pulse data, making it easy to track any fluctuations.

WAGGURACY's real-time pulse monitoring enables you to provide the best care for your pet's cardiovascular habit. By integrating technology with pet care, you can enhance your pet's overall quality of life and well-being. 

Please note that this feature requires **a WAGGURACY device** with pulse monitoring capability and a stable connection to the app. Always consult with a veterinarian for professional guidance on interpreting your pet's pulse data and any potential habit concerns.

# Real-time Location Tracking and Monitoring
WAGGURACY offers advanced **real-time location tracking and monitoring** features designed to ensure the **safety** and **happiness** of your beloved pets. Through these features, you can closely monitor your pet's **movements** and **activities**, ensuring their well-being at all times.

![MAP](/img/map/Map.jpg)

## How to Use:
1. Log in to the **WAGGURACY** App:

    Open the **WAGGURACY** app on your Android or iOS device.

    **log in** to your account.
2. Access Real-time Tracking:

    Tap **Home**.

## Key Features:
In the WAGGURACY app, on the **Home** screen, as long as your pet is wearing PHAST 1 and it is working properly, you can view your pet’s **current location** and **habit data**.

### 1. Monitor Movement - Real-time Tracking:

![realtime tracking](/img/map/activities.jpg)

The map displays your pet's **movement** in **real-time**, enabling you to track their paths and activities.

**Benefits:**

+ Live Location Updates: 

    WAGGURACY offers continuous real-time updates on your pets' locations, allowing you to stay informed about their movements.
+ Immediate Alerts: 

    If your pets leave a designated safe zone, you’ll receive **an alert** on your app, ensuring you can react promptly

    ![fence alert](/img/map/Instant-Fence-Alerts.jpg)

+ Enhanced Safety:

    Whether you're at home or away, you can have peace of mind knowing that you can track your pets and ensure their safety.
+ Customizable Settings:

    Configure the device’s **working mode** according to your preferences and requirements.

**Note:** 

Make sure your device has a stable internet connection and access to GPS signals for accurate and reliable tracking.

### 2. Call Your Pet
WAGGURACY offers an intelligent **Auto Recall** feature designed to make recalling and controlling your pets easier.

Wherever you are, simply tap the recall icon to play a recall sound on the device and guide your pet back safely.

![recall](/img/map/Auto-Recall.jpg)

When you need to recall your pet, simply tap the appropriate button to play a recall sound on the device.

**Benefits:**

+ Instant Recall:

    With Auto Recall, you can instantly play a recall sound on the device to get your pet’s attention and guide them back.
+ Safe Recall:

    Recall your pets remotely no matter where you are to ensure their safe return.
+ Emergency Situations:

    In urgent situations, such as when your pet is lost or in danger, you can convey emergency commands to your pet through the Auto Recall feature.
+ Convenient Usage:

    The interface of the Auto Recall feature is user-friendly, allowing for easy operation.

Additionally, you can also check the **status** of your **device**, including its **battery level**, and **signal strength**. This allows you to stay informed about the habit and performance of your device while using the WAGGURACY app.

![icon](/img/battery/home-battery.jpg)

You can also tap on the icon in the bottom right-hand corner of the map, to zoom to different locations:

![icon](/img/map/botton.jpg)

Each time you tap the icon, a different view will be shown:

| Icon      | Description |
| ----------- |----------- |
| ![N_icon](/img/map/map-north.png) | Click to orient the map according to the north for a clear sense of direction.|
| ![L_icon](/img/map/user.png) | Centers the map on your mobile device's current location.|

**If you are unable to see your pet's current location, please try the following tips:**

+ Double-check that GPS / location services are enabled.
+ Open your phone's Settings menu, then under the location settings, ensure that the WAGGURACY app has permission to use GPS / location services.
+ Update the WAGGURACY GPS app if necessary.
+ Go outside with the Map view open and wait until your PHAST-1 can get a GPS connection.
+ Contact WAGGURACY for further support.

WAGGURACY's real-time location tracking and monitoring features empower you to provide optimal care for your pets. Whether you're at home or away, WAGGURACY ensures that you can always stay connected to, and protect your cherished pets.
